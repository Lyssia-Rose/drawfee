About Drawfee

Drawfee is an art YouTube channel that has been around since 2014 and have amassed a following of over 1.5 million
subscribers. The premise of their channel is they take prompts/suggestions from the comments of their videos and use
them to make dumb drawings in later episodes. Drawfee is very much a channel of artists just having fun drawing and
sharing that love of drawing and comedy with its audience. Drawfee began as just Nathan and Caldwell but now consists
of Nathan, Jacob, Julia, Karina, and David. The Drawfee crew where laid off from CHMedia, a company they were
employed by to make Drawfee as well as other illustration jobs, at the beginning of 2020, however due to the huge
support from the community and their fans, they were able to go independent and have been creating quality videos
since.
